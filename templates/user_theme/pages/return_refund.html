Return & Refund Policy

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer returns under the following conditions:

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery
  • Products must be in original, unused condition
  • Original packaging and tags must be intact
  • Custom-made or personalized items cannot be returned
  • Items showing signs of wear or damage cannot be returned

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team at +91 9667006467
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We will provide return instructions and pickup details
  4. Pack the item securely in original packaging
  5. Our team will inspect the returned item

Refund Policy

Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed after item inspection
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days after receiving returned item
  • Refund amount excludes original shipping charges
  • Return shipping costs are customer's responsibility unless item is defective

Refund Methods

  • COD Orders: Refund via bank transfer or UPI
  • Online Payments: Refund to original payment method
  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • UPI/Net Banking: 3-5 business days

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges are subject to product availability
  • Size/color exchanges allowed within 7 days
  • Price difference (if any) must be paid by customer
  • Exchange shipping charges may apply

Damaged or Defective Items

  • Report damage/defects within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of damaged items
  • We will arrange free pickup and replacement
  • No charges for return shipping of defective items

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-made furniture
  • Personalized items
  • Items damaged by customer misuse
  • Items returned after 7-day window
