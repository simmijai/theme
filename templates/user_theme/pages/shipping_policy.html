Shipping Information

We are committed to delivering your furniture safely and on time. Please review our shipping policy below:

Delivery Process

Delivery Guidelines

  • Customer or authorized person must be present during delivery
  • Inspect items before accepting delivery
  • Report any damage immediately
  • Ensure adequate space for delivery and installation
  • Provide clear directions and contact information

  • Large Furniture: Specialized delivery vehicles
  • Stairs/Elevators: Additional handling charges may apply
  • Remote Locations: Extended delivery time

Tracking Your Order

  • Tracking number provided after dispatch
  • Real-time updates on delivery status
  • Customer service support for tracking queries

Delivery Delays

  • Custom clearance (for imported items)
  • Force majeure events
