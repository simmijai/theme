Payment Policy

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods to make your shopping experience convenient:

1. Cash on Delivery (COD)

  • Pay when your order is delivered to your doorstep
  • Available for orders within our delivery areas
  • No additional charges for COD

2. Online Payment (Coming Soon)

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • UPI Payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • Digital Wallets

Payment Security

Your payment information is secure with us. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your financial data.

Payment Terms

  • Full payment is required before delivery for online payments
  • For COD orders, payment is collected at the time of delivery
  • All prices are inclusive of applicable taxes
  • Prices are subject to change without prior notice

Refund Policy

In case of order cancellation or return, refunds will be processed according to our Return and Refund policy. Refunds for online payments will be credited back to the original payment method within 5-7 business days.
