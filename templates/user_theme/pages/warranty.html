Warranty Policy

Our Warranty Commitment

At Orana Furnitures, we stand behind the quality of our products. Our warranty ensures that you receive furniture that meets our high standards of craftsmanship and durability.

Warranty Coverage

Wooden Furniture

  • Structural Warranty: 2 years against manufacturing defects
  • Finish Warranty: 1 year against peeling, cracking, or fading
  • Hardware: 1 year for hinges, handles, and fittings

Upholstered Furniture

  • Frame: 2 years structural warranty
  • Fabric/Leather: 1 year against manufacturing defects
  • Foam/Cushioning: 1 year against sagging or compression

Metal Furniture

  • Structural: 2 years against welding defects
  • Powder Coating: 1 year against chipping or rust
  • Moving Parts: 6 months for mechanisms

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects in materials
  • Structural failures under normal use
  • Defective hardware or fittings
  • Premature wear of moving parts
  • Finish defects (peeling, cracking)

What's NOT Covered

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Damage from misuse or abuse
  • Accidental damage or spills
  • Damage from pets or children
  • Exposure to extreme conditions
  • Modifications or repairs by unauthorized persons
  • Commercial or heavy-duty use (unless specified)

Warranty Claims Process

  1. Contact Us: Call +91 9667006467 or message on Instagram
  2. Provide Details: Order number, purchase date, and issue description
  3. Photo Evidence: Send clear photos of the defect
  4. Inspection: Our team may schedule an inspection visit
  5. Resolution: Repair, replacement, or refund as applicable

Warranty Terms

  • Warranty starts from the date of delivery
  • Original purchase receipt required for claims
  • Warranty is non-transferable
  • Repairs or replacements don't extend warranty period
  • Transportation costs for warranty service may apply

Care Instructions

To maintain your warranty coverage, please follow these care guidelines:

Wooden Furniture

  • Dust regularly with soft, dry cloth
  • Avoid direct sunlight and heat sources
  • Use coasters and placemats
  • Clean spills immediately
  • Polish occasionally with appropriate wood care products

Upholstered Furniture

  • Vacuum regularly to remove dust and debris
  • Rotate cushions periodically
  • Avoid direct sunlight
  • Clean spills immediately with appropriate cleaners
  • Professional cleaning recommended annually
